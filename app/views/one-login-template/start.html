About GOV.UK accounts
GOV.UK accounts are new. At the moment you can only use your GOV.UK account with a few services.

GOV.UK accounts are currently separate from other government accounts (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).

In the future, you’ll be able to use your GOV.UK account to sign in to most services on GOV.UK.
