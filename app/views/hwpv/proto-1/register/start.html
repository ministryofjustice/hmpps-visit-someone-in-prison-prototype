Get help with the cost of prison visits

Use this service to reclaim the cost of visiting a prisoner in England or Wales. You might be able to get help paying for:

  • travel to the prison
  • overnight accommodation
  • meals

You can apply to get help paying for visits that you have already been on, or have booked for the future.

You must be getting certain benefits or have a health certificate to get help.

If you take a child with you or someone to help you (for example because you’re disabled) you might also be able to get help paying for their visit.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your visit booking reference
  • your National Insurance number
  • any receipts
  • a prison visit form stamped by the prison

You will also need to sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login to use this service.

If you cannot use the online service

If you cannot claim online, you can contact the HMPPS Family Services team by email, phone or post to request an application form.

Help using the service

You can get help with making a claim, ask a question about a claim you’ve already made, or get help with accessing the service.
