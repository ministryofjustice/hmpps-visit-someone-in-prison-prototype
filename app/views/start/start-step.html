Part of Staying in touch with someone in prison

  1. Step 1: Find a prisoner

    Use the Prisoner Location Service to find people in prison when you do not know which prison they are in.

    The prisoner must give their permission for their information to be shared, unless you belong to certain organisations such as the police or a solicitors’ firm.

    1. Find a prisoner

  2. Step 2: Get added as a visitor

    The prisoner will need to add you to their approved visitor list before you can book a visit.

    It can take up to 3 weeks to be added to the approved visitor list.

    1. Apply for your first provisional driving licence £34 to £43

  3. Step 3: Visit someone in prison

    You need a provisional driving licence to take lessons or practice.

  5. Step 5: Go to the visit

    You must pass your theory test before you can book your driving test.

    1. Book your driving test £62 to £75

    3. Change your driving test appointment
    4. Check your driving test appointment details
    5. Cancel your driving test
