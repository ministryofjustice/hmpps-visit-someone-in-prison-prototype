Visit someone in prison

Use this service to:

  • book a face to face visit with a prisoner
  • update your contact details
  • book for other people

Booking takes about 5 minutes.

Start

Before you start

To use this service, you need to:

  • have a visitor account or to create a visitor account
  • be on the prisoner's visitor list
  • not be banned from visiting the prisoner

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

On the day of the visit, you will need to show your ID. This must match the details provided when booking a visit. View acceptable forms of ID

Help with your booking

  • Contact the prison directly
  • Contact the Prisoners' Families Helpline on 0808 808 2003
