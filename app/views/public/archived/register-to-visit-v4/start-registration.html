Register to visit someone at HMP Hewell

To register, you need to submit:

  • the prisoner’s details
  • visitor details

The prison will check the details you submit match the prisoner’s approved visitor list. The prison may also carry out security checks.

Approving registrations typically takes one to three weeks.

When approved, you can book visits without needing to enter prisoner and visitor details again. You will also get text message confirmation of your bookings.
