Providing visitor information

All visitors to prisons in England and Wales, other than accompanied children under the age of 16, are required to prove their identity before entry.

The visitor information you provide must match the acceptable forms of ID the visitor will show when they visit someone in prison.

The visitor must also be on the prisoner's visitor list.

Providing incorrect visitor information or making requests for visitors not on the prisoner's visitor list will result in your request being rejected.
