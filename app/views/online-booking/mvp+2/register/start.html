This new service can be used to visit one prisoner in:

  • Foston Hall (HMP & YOI)
  • Drake Hall (HMP & YOI)

If you need to visit someone in another prison or visit more than one prisoner, contact the prison directly.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the name, prison number and date of birth for the prisoner
  • the names and dates of birth for visitors

You only need to supply this information once. Checks usually take 2 working days but can take up to 5 working days.

After this check, you can book and get instant confirmation of your visit.

You need a GOV.UK One Login to sign in to this service. You can create one if you do not already have one.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Start now", href: "./one-login/create.html", isStartButton: true }) }}

Visits you cannot book online

Contact the prison directly if you need to arrange any of the following:

  • legal visits, for example legal professionals discussing the prisoner’s case
  • reception visits, for example the first visit to the prisoner within 72 hours of being admitted
  • double visits, for example visiting for 2 hours instead of 1
  • family day visits - special family events that the prison organises

Help with the cost of prison visits

You may be able to get help with the cost of prison visits if you’re getting certain benefits or have a health certificate.

Ymweld â rhywun yn y carchar

TGellir defnyddio’r gwasanaeth newydd hwn i ymweld ag un carcharor yn:

  • Foston Hall (HMP & YOI)
  • Drake Hall (HMP & YOI)

Os oes angen i chi ymweld â rhywun mewn carchar arall neu ymweld â mwy nag un carcharor, defnyddiwch y gwasanaeth cais am ymweliad presennol.

Cyn i chi ddechrau

Bydd angen:

  • enw, rhif carchar a dyddiad geni’r
  • carcharor enwau a dyddiadau geni ymwelwyr

Dim ond unwaith y mae angen i chi ddarparu'r wybodaeth hon. Mae gwiriadau fel arfer yn cymryd 2 ddiwrnod gwaith ond gall gymryd hyd at 5 diwrnod gwaith.

Ar ôl y gwiriad hwn, gallwch archebu a chael cadarnhad ar unwaith o'ch ymweliad.

Mae angen One Login GOV.UK arnoch i fewngofnodi i'r gwasanaeth hwn. Gallwch greu un os nad oes gennych un yn barod.

Ymweliadau na allwch eu harchebu ar-lein

Cysylltwch â’r carchar yn uniongyrchol os oes angen i chi drefnu unrhyw un o’r canlynol:

  • ymweliadau cyfreithiol, er enghraifft gweithwyr cyfreithiol proffesiynol yn trafod achos y carcharor
  • ymweliadau derbyn, er enghraifft yr ymweliad cyntaf â’r carcharor o fewn 72 awr i gael ei dderbyn
  • ymweliadau dwbl, er enghraifft ymweld am 2 awr yn lle 1
  • ymweliadau diwrnod teulu - digwyddiadau teuluol arbennig y mae'r carchar yn eu trefnu

Help gyda chost ymweliadau carchar

Efallai y gallwch gael help gyda chost ymweliadau carchar os ydych yn cael budd-daliadau penodol neu os oes gennych dystysgrif iechyd.
