Use this service to:

  • book or request a face to face social visit with a prisoner in England or Wales

It takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You use this service, you need to:

  • have a visitor account or to create a visitor account
  • be on the prisoner's visitor list
  • not be banned from visiting the prisoner
  • know the prisoners number, name and date of birth

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).

On the day of the visit, you will need to show your ID. This must match the details provided when booking a visit.

Help with your booking

If you require further help:

  • contact the prison
  • contact the Prisoners' Families Helpline on 0808 808 2003
