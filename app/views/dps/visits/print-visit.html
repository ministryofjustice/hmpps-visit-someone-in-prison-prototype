Visitor declaration

No unauthorised article may be taken into or from prison or supplied to a prisoner from a visitor. Unauthorised articles may be confiscated; the visitor may be prosecuted, and may on conviction by imprisoned or fined. The prisoner may face a disciplinary charge. Any article brought, with permission, for a prisoner should be handed to the officer supervising visits. Any person entering or leaving a prison may be stopped, searched and photographed. They may also be required to provide a personal biometric (i.e. electronic fingerprint) or submit to another physical measurement.

The Governor may refuse entry to or direct the removal from a prison of any person who does not leave on being required to do so.
